About Naagee Golden Eggs Farm

Our Commitment to Quality

Founded with a passion for providing healthy, nutritious poultry products, Naagee Golden Eggs Farms began operations in 2025 with a commitment to premium quality eggs and chicken. Our farm is located in Accra, Ghana, where our chickens are raised in a clean, well-managed, and stress-free environment.

We believe in sustainable farming practices that prioritize animal welfare and environmental responsibility. Our chickens are fed with natural, hormone-free feed, resulting in products that are not only delicious but also packed with essential nutrients.
